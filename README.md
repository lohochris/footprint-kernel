An enterprise-grade **Privacy Intelligence & Risk Audit System** designed to help UK citizens quantify, visualize, and mitigate their digital exposure. Built on the **Muhammad et al. Resilience Framework**, this platform provides real-time telemetry on data leakage and cognitive privacy literacy.

---

## Core Features

- **Intelligence-Led Risk Audit**: Multi-category assessment covering Security, Social, Data, and Legal exposure.
- **Dynamic Risk Score**: Real-time calculation of digital perimeter vulnerability using weighted telemetry.
- **Cognitive Literacy Engine**: Evaluates user awareness against modern social engineering and cyber-threat vectors.
- **Privacy Archetype Classification**: Categorizes users into distinct behavioral typologies to provide targeted action plans.
- **Strategy Room**: A high-fidelity mitigation ledger with mandatory protocols for perimeter hardening.
- **Exportable Assets**: Generate comprehensive PDF Audit Reports and raw JSON telemetry payloads.

---

## The Framework

This system implements the **Muhammad et al. Privacy Resilience Model (2026)**. Unlike traditional checklists, the Footprint Manager utilizes:

1.  **Weighted Domain Distribution**: Prioritizing high-impact vulnerabilities (e.g., credential management, multi-cloud leakage).
2.  **Predictive Mitigation**: Modeling a 70%+ reduction in risk upon successful deployment of the Strategy Room action plan.
3.  **Behavioral Typology**: Classifying users (e.g., *Privacy Pragmatists*, *Data Minimalists*) to ensure advice is contextually relevant.
